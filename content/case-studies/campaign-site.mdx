## Challenge

Launch a time-boxed campaign with a premium editorial feel while staying performant on mobile networks.

## What we shipped

Scroll-linked typography, restrained animation, and image treatments that stayed sharp at every breakpoint.

## Learnings

Pairing display type with generous whitespace outperformed dense layouts in engagement tests.
