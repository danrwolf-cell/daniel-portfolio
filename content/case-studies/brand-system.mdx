## Context

The team needed a single coherent system that could flex across marketing, product, and in-clinic touchpoints without fragmenting.

## Approach

We anchored on a modular type ramp, a restrained two-color palette with one accent, and motion rules that felt calm rather than flashy.

## Outcomes

- Unified design tokens shipped in Figma and code
- Faster handoffs with fewer one-off screens
- Stronger brand recognition in user research sessions
